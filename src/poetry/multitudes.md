Stylesheets to stylestreets,
No vest to invest,
Well-heeled feet,
Writing wicked tests. 
I am ‘and,’ but not ‘or.’
Don’t try to make me less,
When I know I’m more.

Say what you want, my dudes,
But I contain multitudes.
